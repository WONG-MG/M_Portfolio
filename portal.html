Project Title: Portal App

PORTAL IS A GAMING PLATFORM OF BLOCKCHAIN
PORTAL allows for link between game & GameFi and converges a variety of game&gamefi into a single economic model, as well as being able to access & collect game data with ease. PORTAL also has massive amount of game resources and provides its user with rich & engaging gaming experience.

1. Product Definition

Before I build a product, I need to understand its context for existence as the product definition is an important phase as it sets the foundation for the final product. This phase usually include: Interviewing key stakeholders to gather insights about their needs and goals, thinking about the key aspects and the value of the product: what it is, who will use it and why they will use it.

This low fidelity wireframe mock-up was created using Balsamiq software

Creating an early mockup of the product (can be low-fidelity). This phase typically ends up with a project kick-off meeting. The kick-off meeting brings all the key players together to set proper expectations both for the product team and stakeholders.

2. User Research

Once I define the idea, I move on to the User Research Phase. In this phase, I will be asking users about their experience with a product, and observing how users interact with the product in their environment.

This User Journey map I made shows how a previous gaming experience has shaped the awareness and the way of perceiving games by a given user and how it has influenced his decisions. Such analysis of thinking, doing and feeling helps give them negative and positive feedbacks from using our platforms.

To start this process, I went to research on external game sources to get expose to different types of game platforms and gain a deeper understanding and insights of the products

5. Validation

The last phase is validation (testing). Usually the testing phase starts after the high-fidelity design is ready. During a series of user testing sessions, the team validates the product with both stakeholders and end-users to ensure that their expectations is fulfilled

This was a project assigned from China clients using Figma software. I went to research a lot of UI game designs from many websites and played multiple games to gain a better understanding of the user flow. I started with designing the Wireframes, followed by applying multiple designs and colour schemes for clients to choose. After clients have confirmed their choice, I started to design the logos and complete all UI Designs and prototypes. Lastly, after all the designs have been completed, I also demonstrated to clients to explain the overall user flow and designs. During this project, I have worked closely with the developers to discuss about issues encountered along the way. Once developers have completed some pages, I will proceed with the checking of alignments and ensure testing of user flow are correct and accurate.
