Project Title: HomiEx

HomiEx is a Singapore-based digital asset exchange company that provides security and professional services for its global users. Since its establishment in 2017, HomiEx has accumulated users from 39 countries, offering spots and derivative trading services for high quality digital assets with good liquidity. Besides fiat-to-crypto OTC service, HomiEx also supports direct deposits and withdraws of 8 major currencies including CNY, AUD, HKD, SGD, AUD, JPY, USD, EUR and GBP.

My Role

One of the main projects I worked on at HomiEx is editing the concepts of the website. Our small team worked closely with marketing to co-create solutions that solved real user needs. I was responsible for designing new pages for their current site and I come up with wireframes and flowcharts referencing from used cases.

My Process

  • - Explore design options, brainstorm with Front-end Developer and engineers. For workflow-heavy features, come up with wireframes and flowcharts driven by use cases; For reports, charts, data grids and other modules, explore different visualization options. Optimize for clarity and actionable insights.
    •
  • - Work with engineers on edge cases, error states, loading & empty states etc. Make sure designs are consistent with the design system.
    •
  • - Build prototypes to show end-to-end flows or micro-interactions.
    •
  • - Provide assets and specs. QA and work with engineers on fixes and tweaks.
    •
  • - After launching the feature, work with PMs to define enhancements and opportunities, come up with improved designs and work with engineers to implement them.
