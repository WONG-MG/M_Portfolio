Project Title: UOB KayHian

This project was from a UOB KayHian Market News and I completed the Market News page and Event Calender page using Figma Software. Aside from designs, I have also completed responsive designs for desktop and mobile sizes and demonstrated to clients.

I started structuring my design process following the design thinking approach, until the last stage of validation.

Design Process

User Research

Interview

Through the interview, it shows that the majority of people wants to read the stock market news daily.

The image below depicts the average percentage of the respondent's response that took part in this survey.

User Needs

  1. Simplicity
    Simplicity UI makes the website more user friendly, easy to use and less complicated

  2. Customization
    Customization allow users to make limited modifications to the UI based on personal preference

  3. Convenience
    Convenience is intended to make users more comfortable for a long time and easier to user the UI

  4. Management
    Management means organizing and structuring the UI so that it is neat and organized

User Persona

I took 2 users who I felt could represent the opinions. I identified these two people were, their pain and gain, along with the flow they took when they wanted to know the stock markets news.

Market Research

This survey showed that more of the people are using the internet and mobile to check out the stock markets news.
