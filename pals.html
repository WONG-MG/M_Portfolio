Project Title: PALS APPLICATION

What is PALS?
To help seniors to age-in-place by providing personalised care for seniors
to better manage their health and be supported by local communities to live independently.

Purpose of PALS Application

  1. Serve as a Trusted Organiser for Seniors to Manage Schedule
    View to-dos and events for the day in a single view

  2. Personal Wellness Assistant to Support Seniors Health
    To be reminded of their personalized care tasks Better manage chronic conditions with useful tips and the support of a care team

  3. Events Explorer for Seniors to Discover Events
    Discover resources and join in events that are happening to widen social circle with Active Ageing Centre(AAC)

Suite of Solutions

1. Gather Care Admin System
AAC staff, Community Nurses

  • Upload events and care plan
  • Review health monitoring data collected
  • Provide support to residents when needed

2. PALS Tablet
Seniors enrolled into this trial

  • View consolidated schedule for the day with reminders
  • Discover events and resources
  • Have a teleconsult with your primary care provider
  • Available in 4 languages(EN/CN/MY/TL)

3. Sensei Care Kit
Seniors enrolled into this trial

Use the health monitoring devices to have the readings automatically synced to PALS for the care team to review

Care Kit Includes:

  • Blood Pressure Monitor
  • Tablet Mount
  • Medication Drawer
  • Easy to carry design
